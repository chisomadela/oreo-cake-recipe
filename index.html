The Softest Oreo Cake Recipe

If you're a cookies and cream lover,then you are going to literally fall in love with this incredibly soft oreo cake! this recipe consists of soft vanilla and oreo cake layers,topped with a super smooth oreo buttercream frosting

Preparation Time

  • Total: 1hr 40mins
  • Preparation: 40mins
  • Baking: 40mins
  • Frosting: 20mins

Ingredients

  • 14 Oreos-roughly chopped
  • 300g flour-regular all purpose
  • 25g cornflour
  • half tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • half tsp salt-omit if using salted butter
  • 113g unsalted butter-room temperature
  • 105g unflavoured vegetable oil
  • 332g white granulated sugar
  • 3 large eggs
  • one-quarter tbsp vanilla extract
  • 1 tsp white vinegar
  • 337g buttermilk-room temperature

Ingredients for oreo buttercream frosting

  • 14 Oreos-blended to fine crumbs
  • 340g unsalted butter-room temperature
  • 350g icing sugar
  • one and half tsp vanilla extract
  • one and half tbsp milk


Instructions

  1. Preheat oven: Begin by preheating your oven to 160C(320F) with the fan on and grease and/or line two 8 inch cake tins
  2. Chop oreos: Next you want to roughly chop 14 oreos to mix into the cake batter at the end.You don't want them to be too big or too small(you want little chucks)
  3. Sift your dry ingredients: In a bowl,sift together your flour,cornflour,baking soda,baking powder and salt.Using a whisk or fork,mix until well combined.Set aside.
  4. Cream butter and sugar: In another bowl,add in your butter,vegetable oil and sugar.Using an electric mixer cream together for 2 minutes until light and creamy.
  5. Add the eggs: Add in eggs one by one one,mixing well in between each addition(about 10-15 seconds between eggs).
  6. Add the extract and buttermilk: Add in your vanilla,vinegar and half a cup of the buttermilk(112g),and mix until well combined.Now set your mixer aside as the remainder of the batter will be finished of the batter will be finished by hand
  7. Add in your dry ingredients: strong Next add in half of your premixed dry ingredients to your wet mixture,and gently fold it in with a spatula until just combined.then add in the remaining 1 cup of buttermilk(225g),and fold it in until just combined.Lastly,add in the remaining dry ingredients and gently fold it into the mixture until just combined.Do not over mix
  8. Roughly chop oreos: Finish off by adding in the roughly chopped oreos from earlier and gently mix with the spatula until just combined.Do not over mix
  9. Distribute and bake: Distribute the batter evenly into the two inch cake tins,and bake for 40 minutes or until a toothpick comes out clean
  10. Once baked:Once baked,allow the cake layers to cool in the cake tins for about 15-20 minutes,and then turn them out onto a wire rack to completely cool.
